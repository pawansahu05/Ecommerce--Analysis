# 🛒 E-commerce Sales & Customer Analysis
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to identify customer behavior, sales trends, profitability patterns, and overall business performance insights.

Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, raw transactional data was transformed into meaningful business intelligence dashboards and analytical reports.

# 🎯 Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data, making it difficult to identify:

High-performing products
Profitable customer segments
Revenue-driving regions
Seasonal sales patterns
Profitability trends
This project helps uncover actionable business insights through data analysis and visualization.

# 🔍 Key Insights
Certain regions contributed significantly higher revenue compared to others.
Some product categories generated high sales but lower profit margins.
Monthly sales trends revealed noticeable seasonal spikes.
Customer purchasing behavior varied across categories and regions.
# 💡 Business Recommendations
Increase focus on high-performing regions.
Improve profitability in low-margin categories.
Optimize inventory during high-demand periods.
Strengthen customer retention strategies.
